Safety tips:

Here follows some general safety tips on how to prevent getting sick and/or spreading the COVID-19 virus! And please adhere to your country's lock-down regulations!

  • Practice basic hygiene.
  • Wash your hands and up to your elbows as often as possible.
  • Practice social distancing of at least 1m (8 feet) even after lock-down is lifted.
  • Use hand-sanitizer.
  • When going out for critical supplies, wear a face mask and/or surgical gloves.
  • Do not touch you eyes, nose or mouth before sanitizing or washing your hands.
